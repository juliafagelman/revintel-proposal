Revintel Product Documentation Sprint

Foundational Product Guide & Knowledge Architecture
Prepared for: Revintel
Prepared by: Julia Fagelman
Date: 3 March 2026

Overview

Revintel delivers real-time pipeline visibility and AI-driven revenue insights through executive dashboards and conversational AI.

This sprint establishes a centralized documentation foundation that clearly explains:

  • How the system works
  • How dashboards and AI insights should be interpreted
  • How data is calculated and refreshed
  • How customers can use Revintel effectively

Objective

  • Clearly articulate Revintel’s value and functionality
  • Centralize product logic outside the UI
  • Support customer onboarding and understanding
  • Define key metrics and data assumptions
  • Structure documentation for future AI retrieval systems

Scope of Work

Phase 1: Discovery & Architecture (2 days)

  • Product deep dive session
  • Clarification of value proposition and workflows
  • Identification of key metrics and data logic
  • Documentation structure design

Phase 2: Foundational Product Guide (5 days)

  • Product Overview
  • System Overview
  • Dashboard Guide
  • AI Insights & Chat Guide
  • Metrics & Methodology
  • FAQ

Light screenshots may be included selectively for clarity, but this sprint does not include full step-by-step UI walkthrough guides.

Deliverables

  • Complete Foundational Product Documentation (structured and modular)
  • AI-ready terminology consistency
  • One structured revision round
